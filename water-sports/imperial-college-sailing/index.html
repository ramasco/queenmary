Welcome to Imperial Sailing based at Queen Mary Sailing Club for the 2025-26 season.

Activities and Events at QMSC:

  • Team Racing Wednesday and Saturday (Watch us on YouTube)
  • Opportunities to learn to sail
  • Keelboat Sailing on RS21s
  • The Imperial Icicle (BUSA Event)

The Club

Imperial College Sailing Club prides itself in our inclusivity, as we host all levels of sailing. We train every Saturday from 9am until 4pm and host theory sessions on campus every other Wednesday, followed by a social evening and team bonding!

Last year the Imperial team competed at BUSA, getting two teams into Finals, as well as attending competitions in the London area and at Oxford, Cambridge, Reading and other universities.

We sail at the renowned Queen Mary Sailing Club, known for its high level sailing, offering us the opportunity to train alongside other experienced alumni teams! All members have access to club boats so you can sail whenever you want.

So, if you want to learn or improve, compete at universities over the country, be part of a team, escape central London and make lots of new friends then this is the club for you.

Contact us on sailing@ic.ac.uk or message us on Instagram or Facebook to find out more!
