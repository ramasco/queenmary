Wing Festival

Thank you to everyone attending WINGFEST... we will be back in 2026!

London’s Wing Festival Returns to Queen Mary Sailing Club

The highly anticipated London Wing Festival is set to return to Queen Mary Sailing Club on Sunday, 18th May. This event will bring together enthusiasts, industry experts and top brands for a day dedicated to wing surfing and wing foiling.

Wing Fest is a free event - Sign Up Now 

Event Highlights

  • Try the Latest Equipment – You will have the opportunity to test the latest wing surfing and wing foiling gear from leading brands.
  • Meet the Experts – Experts from across the sport will be available to discuss the latest trends, advancements and techniques.
  • Coaching and Skill Development – Whether you're an experienced rider or new to the sport, the event will feature interactive coaching sessions designed to enhance your skills and understanding of the sport.

Jerome Schmitt, our Chief Windsurfing Instructor and the driving force behind Wing Festival, expressed his enthusiasm for the upcoming event:

"This is our second Wing-Fest, bringing together top brands, coaches, and wing specialists from across the UK. We are excited to see our partners unite again for a fantastic day at Queen Mary. With the sport’s growing popularity, we aim to make this event accessible and enjoyable for all participants."

Jerome will lead the coaching team, ensuring all attendees receive expert guidance and support throughout the day.

Beginner Sessions – Introduction to Winging

For those new to the sport, a two-hour beginner session will be available for £45, with a 25% discount for club members. These sessions will provide a wonderful introduction to winging, covering essential techniques and safety practices.  

Participation and Registration

  • Join us for the Wing Festival Entry is FREE! Registration here
  • Booking link below for a Free Day Sailing at Wing Festival - to bring your own kit to hit the water.! 

Additional Attractions - The event will also feature:

  • Wing coaching with Simon Winkley Marine 
  • Food and refreshments provided by Claire Cooks Surrey
  • Club members will also be available to talk about Winging at QMSC if you wish to learn more about joining Queen Mary. 

London Wing Festival 2025 promises to be an exciting day for both experienced riders and newcomers to the sport.

We look forward to seeing you!

Wing Fest – Sunday 18th May | Joining Instructions

We’re looking forward to welcoming you to Wing Fest on Sunday, 18th May for a full day of winging, learning, and fun at Queen Mary Sailing Club.

Event Schedule
Time: 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM
Venue: Queen Mary Sailing Club
Gates Open: 09:00 AM

Registration
Please check in at the registration desk as soon as you arrive. Location: Under the sheltered area on the water side, next to the rigging area.
All participants must sign in before joining any activities and watch the safety briefing.

Water Access – Day Sail Form Required
If you are planning to go on the water, you must complete a Day Sail form. This can be done in advance here:
https://www.queenmary.org.uk/book/add/p/416

Coaching Sessions with Simon Winkley
Simon Winkley will be offering land-based drop-in coaching sessions at 11:30 AM, 1:00 PM, 2:30 PM, and 4:00 PM. Each session will last around 20 minutes and include practical tips and advice. Simon will also be out on the water throughout the day taking photos, which will be uploaded after the event at:
https://swmarine.smugmug.com/

Exhibitors
Exhibitors will be showcasing the latest kit and innovations in the sport throughout the day. It’s a great opportunity to discover new trends and see how the sport is developing.

Catering
Claire Cooks and our catering team will be serving food from the barbecue starting at 12:00 PM.

Parking and Access
Please note that space at the top is limited due to all the activity. Follow signs for drop-off at the top. Cars must be parked at the bottom car park. Arriving early is recommended.

WING Taster Session (DISCOVER WING-SURFING)

Start Winging with QM's Discover-Wing Sessions for those over 18. 

These introductory adult sessions offers a foundational understanding of winging, divided into two main segments: Flying the Wing Ashore and Getting Afloat. These sessions focus on mastering the basics of wing control, harnessing its power for forward movement and steering.

Additionally, they reinforce essential skills such as board balance, enabling participants to navigate short distances across the wind, execute gybes, and return to their starting point confidently.
