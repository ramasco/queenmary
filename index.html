Welcome to Queen Mary

West London Sailing • Windsurfing • Wing-foiling • Keelboating • Kayaking & Canoeing

Set on a raised, open reservoir in West London, Queen Mary Sailing Club (QMSC) is the ultimate destination for watersports enthusiasts across London and the South East. As an RYA Training Centre and a not-for-profit sports club, we’re passionate about making sailing, windsurfing, keelboating and wingfoiling accessible to all.

At QMSC, we believe your leisure time should be hassle-free. With a professional team in place, members are not required to do club duties — just pure watersports enjoyment! Whether you’re a seasoned sailor or a total beginner, our extensive fleet of boats and boards, expert tuition, and vibrant community ensure there’s something for everyone.

We are very lucky to have a wet bar and café on site, run by Claire Cooks Surrey. They offer fresh, hot food and drinks and can cater for all your catering requirements. Click here to view the menu.

New Members Welcome!

We extend our warmest greetings and invite new members to join our vibrant and inclusive sailing community. If you're searching for a club that combines thrilling races, enjoyable social sailing, and top-notch facilities, training and coaching, your quest ends here!

At QMSC, located in West London, we pride ourselves on creating a welcoming environment where sailors of all levels can flourish. Whether you're a seasoned sailor or just beginning to explore the joys of sailing, our club offers a fantastic platform for you to indulge in your passion, learn new skills, and forge lasting friendships along the way.

One of our greatest strengths lies in our racing scene. With a strong racing ethos and a highly competitive fleet, we guarantee the best action on the water. Whether you prefer dinghy racing, keelboat racing, or windsurfing, our range of racing events from Rookie racing to Championship events caters to all interests and abilities.

Beyond the exciting racing opportunities, we understand the importance of fostering a sense of camaraderie within our community. Our Saturday Club social sailing program provides a perfect avenue for members to explore the beauty of sailing in a relaxed and convivial atmosphere.

In addition to our engaging sailing activities, we take immense pride in our comprehensive range of facilities — over 100 boats, excellent training resources, and marine style pontoons. With our knowledgeable instructors and supportive community, you'll have the opportunity to develop your skills and gain confidence on the water.

So, if you're seeking a club that embodies the perfect blend of racing, training, enjoyable social sailing, and outstanding facilities, look no further than QMSC. Join us and become part of a community that shares your love for the wind.

We can't wait to welcome you aboard! — The QM Team.
