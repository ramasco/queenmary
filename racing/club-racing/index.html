Club Racing

Looking to get into Club Racing? We run a Rookie Racing Series on various Saturday afternoons throughout the spring, summer and autumn. Club Racing takes place every Sunday morning throughout the year and is complemented by a Wednesday Evening Series through the spring and summer. See here for dates, Notices of Race and Sailing Instructions plus links to sign on.

Wednesday Evening Series

The Wednesday Evening Series starts on April 8th and consists of 22 races, one every Wednesday, until September 2nd. All club members are welcome, please read the Notice of Race and Sailing Instructions

Click here to sign-on for the entire series. Please notify the office in person if you change rig size, sail number, or boat class/crew before heading out on the water.

Click here to sign-on for the entire series. Please notify the office in person if you change rig size, sail number, or boat class/crew before heading out on the water.

Rookie Racing

Rookie Racing is intended for those Club Members new to sailing or new to racing who want to gain experience of how club racing is run in an easy and friendly environment. The format is similar to our Wednesday Evening Series and racing is run from the Club's race committee boat. There is a prize for each series winner, but you can only win a Rookie Racing series prize once! Gather on the waterside in front of the clubhouse at 13:30hrs for a race briefing with a first start at 14:30hrs and finishing at 16:30hrs.

To join in please sign up for the Rookie Racing here. No need to sign on each week but if you change boat please sign on again. Remember to book your club boat beforehand and please read the Sailing Instructions.

Rookie Racing Dates 2026

Spring Series – 25th April, 9th May, 23th May

Summer Series – 6th June, 20th June, 4th July

Autumn Series – 12th September, 26th September, 10th October
