Frequently Asked Questions

  • Booking 3 or more people on the same course?

    A 10% discount is available on sailing, windsurfing, keelboating and multi-activity courses, do call us on 01784 248881 for more information

  • Course full?

    If the course you are interested in is full, do give us a call and we can see if we can increase the spaces available

  • Do I need to be able to swim?

    You need to be confident in the water, but there is no minimum swimming ability required

  • What do you provide?

    We provide wetsuit, buoyancy aid and all sailing equipment; For Open Training events, you would normally have your own equipment and fees do not cover use of QM kit

  • What should I bring with me?

    Shoes you are happy to get wet, swimwear to wear under your wetsuit, towel, a change of clothes, suntan cream and a hat

  • What is the minimum number of students for a course to run?

    Usually we need 3 people to run a course, we will contact you if we do not reach that number

  • What happens if I need to cancel?

    You may cancel any time up until 14 days before the course (instructor courses require a minimum of 2 month notice) with full refund (minus the admin fee). After that time, there is a 100% fee

  • What qualifications do your staff hold?

    All our RYA courses are taught by qualified RYA Instructors inline with RYA guidelines. All non-RYA courses are taught by appropriately trained Instructors in line with our AALA recognition.

  • More questions?

    Just give us a call on 01784 248881

  • How do I redeem a voucher?

    Select Redeem a Voucher and then follow instructions to validate the voucher.  You will need the voucher code to do this. Next, select courses and start to book your chosen course; the value of your voucher will automatically be deducted from the total cost of that course.  Call us on 01784 248881 at any point if you need assistance.
