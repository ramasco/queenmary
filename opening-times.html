‘Windy Evenings’ will be possible on Mondays, Tuesdays, Thursdays and Fridays. They will be triggered when, at midday on the day before, www.windguru.cz predicts 15 knots or more for 1900, and the club has available staff to cover the late opening.

Times for Windy evenings will be:

April — Off-Water = 1900, Gates Close = 1930

May — Off-Water = 2000, Gates Close = 2030

June — Off-Water = 2000, Gates Close = 2030

July — Off-Water = 2000, Gates Close = 2030

August — Off-Water = 2000, Gates Close = 2030

September — Off-Water = 1900, Gates Close = 1930

NB. Off-Water times may be brought forward in the event of poor light conditions or inclement weather to ensure the safety of all sailors.

“Windies” will be announced on our website, on our facebook page, on Twitter @QMWind

Please note for late opening on weekends and windy evenings, if the water is empty 60mins before advertised offwater time, the club will close earlier than scheduled. If you intend to bring your boat up after this time, please let the office know — 01784 248881.

Extended Opening Hours

We extend the opening hours if the wind is up and we can provide suitable safety cover. We will let you know via the home page of our website and Facebook QueenMarySC.

Windy Mondays and Tuesdays

We will aim to open on Mondays and/or Tuesdays if, on the Sunday afternoon before, www.windguru.cz predicts an average of 15 knots or more for each respective day and subject to staff availability. Announcements will be made on the Sunday before on our website and on our facebook page.

Please note that staff availability, particularly around the busy Christmas/New Year period, may mean we cannot open for a Windy even if the conditions are favourable.

Times for Windy Mondays and Tuesdays are:

Open 10:00 — Off-water 15:00 — Gates Closed 15:30

Please note that off-water times may be revised in the event of poor light conditions or inclement weather. Windy Mondays and Tuesdays may not be offered if conditions are looking unfavourable.
