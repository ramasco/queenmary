Join us!

Hello and Welcome to Queen Mary! Membership of the Club is open to all. The Club is run by professional staff and offers a friendly welcome along with a packed programme of activities. Why not give us a try!

Membership at Queen Mary

  • No Membership Duties - QM Members are not required to perform any duties.
  • No Tide, No River Flow - Queen Mary is as big as the sea, without all the hassle of tides or river currents.
  • No Kit required (Optional) - Choose our unique Select Membership for access to dinghies, windsurf equipment and keelboats.
  • 25% Discount - Members Save a whopping 25% on RYA Training Course, events and activities run by Queen Mary!
  • Clubhouse Facilities – Hot showers, changing facilities, bar café by Claire Cooks Surrey, rigging space, safety cover, and the club is run by a professional team with expert advice readily available.
  • Boat Park and Board Cage Spaces - We have plenty of boat park spaces and Windsurf storage available.

See below for more details about our different membership options. To find out more and get started please call us on 01784 248881 or email us at sailondon@queenmary.org.uk

Start your QM Experience!

Five ways to join...

Standard Membership the traditional dinghy, windsurf or wing owner membership. Covers the use of all shore and water facilities by a family or an individual. The membership year starts 1st April and paid by monthly instalments with a rolling contract. All with No Joining Fee and No club duties required.

QM Select is a Gym style monthly membership with fantastic package enabling you to sail dinghies, keelboats and/or go windsurfing. Using the clubs extensive range of kit whenever the club is open. For those new to sailing there is a package to include tuition, our hire only option is for those Level 2 or equivalent experience, hassle free sailing is the life!

Youth Select making youth sailing affordable without the need to buy any kit. Youth Select gives to access to sailing over fifty boats across seven different classes! It includes all weekend activities developing sailing skills, getting into racing, making long lasting friendships and having fun!

Day Membership suits those who have their own kit which they may store at home but want the flexibility of being able to come up to the Club and use the facilities on an occasional basis.

Boat and Board Hire Experienced sailors are invited to turn up and can hire our kit. So you could be Sailing or Windsurfing or SUP at Queen Mary sooner than you expected! Terms and conditions apply and sailing is at the discretion of the safety team.

There are plenty of opportunities to help with the running of the Club through volunteering to help on a regular basis with Race Management, Youth Group, Sailability and the Club's Council. There are also one-off opportunities to help with big events such as the Bloody Mary which takes place at the beginning of January each year.

To find out more about Membership email taina.greenaway@queenmary.org.uk or call 01784 248881

Insurance

All Standard club members shall have adequate third party liability cover, at least £3M, to cover their own equipment.
