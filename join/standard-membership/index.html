Standard Membership 

APPLICATION FORM

Direct Debit Form

QMSC offers membership to people wishing to 'Boat' in dinghies, keelboats and catamarans or 'Board' on Windsurfs, Wind-foils and Wing-foils.

Standard membership covers the use of all shore and water facilities by a family or an individual. On joining the Club, a member is eligible to vote at the AGM, and to join in all activities at the Club appropriate to their membership category. The membership year starts 1st April running through to 31st March and members can pay either by Direct Debit (DD) or Paid in Full (PIF), DD allowing the option to pay over 12 months PIF annual fees are due on 1st April.

  • There is no joining fees
  • Your are free from compulsory duties - none required!
  • We're open 7 days a week in the Summer
  • Benefit 25% discount on courses run at QMSC
  • Open Wed-Sun in the Winter - with extended opening hours when the wind is up

Membership fees can be reduced by volunteering as a Race Officer and completing a number of RO duties

All club members shall have adequate third party liability cover, at least £3M. For windsurfers and wingfoilers the RYA provides FREE third Party cover when you take RYA Membership, find out more about RYA membership benefits here.

  • Click here for Safety tips for Wing-foiling

Categories and Rates to 31st March 2027:

Full - £46.00 per month

Sailors, windsurfers and wingfoiliers who are 30 years and above in the current year.

Family - £65.00 per month

Covers the use of all shore and water facilities by a full member, their partner and all children under the age of 18 years in the current year.

Junior - £17 per month

Covers the use of all shore and water facilities by a person who is under the age of 18 years in the current year (non voting).

Student - £17 per month

Membership for those in full-time education over 18 years of age.

Crew Pass - £26 per month

Available to Full or Family members with boats to provide full facilities to an unnamed adult crew.

Crew - £26 per month

Members who are 30 years and above in the current year and who will not normally be taking the helm when sailing. Usually linked to a boat and or full member.

Young Adult - £17 per month

Members between the ages of 18 and 30 who are not in full time education

Social - £5 per month

Covers the use of shore side facilities by a person who has attained the age of 18 years in the current year (non-voting).

 

Berthing and Board Racks

  • Dinghy Berth £24 per month
  • Keelboat Berth £27 per month
  • Catamaran Berth £27 per month
  • Windsurfing Secure Rack: Large £24 per month
  • Windsurfing Secure Rack: Standard £20 per month

Family Membership 

At Queen Mary, we are proud to offer a wider range of membership solutions options designed to support every stage of your family’s sailing journey. Our Standard Family Membership provides exceptional value for kit-owning families, encompassing two adults and all children aged 18 or under for Boat, Board, or Wingfoil activities. This membership grants full access to our facilities and the freedom to enjoy time on the water together.

For families seeking access to club equipment with coaching included, our Select Membership offers a unique activity driven solution. Select Membership includes training, coaching, and use of club kit, making it an ideal choice for younger sailors or those progressing their skills. Many families enjoy a blended structure... such as parents sailing their own boat under Standard Membership while children benefit from the Pay & Play Select pathway, with weekend coaching included. This flexible combination ensures each family member enjoys the right level of support, development, and access at an exceptionally competitive price.

We understand that every family is different and our team is dedicated to helping you find the most effective and affordable membership arrangement for your needs. Whether you are experienced sailors or taking your first steps onto the water, Queen Mary provides a welcoming environment, expert guidance and an unbeatable community.

We invite you to visit us, meet our friendly team, and discover the best membership solution for your family. Come and see how Queen Mary can help you make the most of your time on the water together.

Member Guest 

At Queen Mary Sailing Club we warmly welcome guests of our members. A member is allowed six guest passes per year, may bring no more than two guests on any single day, and a guest may use the club’s facilities a maximum of three times in a year (no matter who invites them). Club racing is reserved for members and a guest can participate one time per calendar year.

Whether hosting friends, family, or overseas visitors, members are encouraged to share the enjoyment of our Club’s facilities in a safe and responsible manner. To ensure a positive experience for all, the following Guest Policy applies:

Eligibility to Bring Guests Reserved for our fee-paying non-temporary members and honorary life members are permitted to invite guests to the Club, and they are known as the host member.

Guest Registration all guests must be registered in advance via this online Guest Registration form, which is found on the Club website.
