ADULT Courses

We are one of the leading RYA sailing schools in the UK, specialising in RYA sailing courses, RYA windsurfing courses, RYA keelboat courses, RYA instructor training, and RYA powerboat courses. We have been helping people learn to sail for over 50 years by providing a full range of sailing courses and lessons. Our courses are delivered in a friendly yet professional environment by experienced instructors who make your sailing lessons both fun and memorable.

  • Group Booking Save 10% - Booking three or more courses
  • Standard Members Save 25% - Maximum saving for members when booking is 25%

Get the Family on the Water!
We offer bespoke family sessions tailored to suit your needs. Whether you're looking for an adventurous day out or working towards qualifications, we've got you covered. Give us a call or send us an email to find out more – we'd love to help plan your perfect family experience!
