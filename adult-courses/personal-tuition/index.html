Personal Tuition

One to One is a great way to learn, refresh and develop your skills in windsurfing and sailing. It's just you and the instructor working together on the water to achieve your goals, here are a few:

  • You want to start Sailing or Windsurfing, and prefer one to one tuition.
  • You've been off the water for a number of years and want a boost to refresh your skills.
  • You're a sailor or windsurfer who wants to Start Foiling, take flight at QMSC.

If you have any issues, please get in contact by email to sailondon@queenmary.org.uk.
