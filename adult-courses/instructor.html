Become a Qualified RYA Instructor

Are you passionate about sailing, windsurfing or keelboats and eager to share your skills with others? Join the RYA Instructor Courses and turn your love for the water into a rewarding teaching experience! Note - the minimum age for courses is 16.

Booking Discounts:

  • Group Booking of 2 or more - SAVE 10%
  • Members Automatic Maximum Saving - SAVE 25%

About the Training Course:

  • Duration: Typically 50 hours over 5 days, or modular over weekends/days.
  • Comprehensive training in teaching techniques, both afloat and ashore.
  • Supervised by experienced RYA Coaches/Assessors with moderation on the final day.
  • Focus on developing your skills in course preparation, delivery, management, and customer interaction.

Click on the course links below to find out more.
