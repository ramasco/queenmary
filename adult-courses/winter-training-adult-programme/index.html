Adult Winter Training Programme

Don’t stop sailing or windsurfing in the winter months! We have our ‘Winter Training Programme’ to keep you on the water and developing your skills until the season restarts in Spring. You could also take advantage and try something new... This could be your year to get into club racing; if you're already on the start line maybe try team-racing!

The winter training programme runs from October - March every year

Courses are FREE for 'Select Full' members and a 25% Discount for all others club members.
