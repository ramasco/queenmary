Positions Vacant

All instructor roles subject to satisfactory references and an enhanced DBS check.

LEAD INSTRUCTOR 

Employment Type: Full-time, Seasonal (March-November) 

Salary: £27,300 (pro rata for the period 1st March-31st October 2026) 

Benefits: 

  • Unlimited use of club kit 

  • Storage for personal dinghies/boards 

  • All relevant CPD/instructor endorsements funded 

  • Discounts on QM courses for friends/family members 

About Us: QMSC is a large, active RYA Affiliated Sailing Club and RYA Recognised Training Centre. We offer club racing, open events, club activities, and RYA tuition in sailing, windsurfing, winging, and powerboating throughout the year. 

Role Overview: We are seeking a motivated and individual to join our team as Lead Instructor. The successful candidate will have a drive for staff mentoring and leadership, with the ability to inspire instructors in their continued development. This role requires a flexible approach, with a minimum of 35 hours per week, including weekends, evenings, and bank holidays as required. The typical working week is Wednesday to Sunday in winter, with variable days in summer. 

Application Process: To apply, please send a CV and covering letter to Ross Malloy by Sunday, 15th February at sailing.principal@queenmary.org.uk. Please note any offer of employment is subject to satisfactory DBS checks and references. 

 

Experience & Qualifications: 

Essential: 

  • RYA Senior Instructor with a valid First Aid certificate 

  • Minimum of 1 year’s experience working in a Recognised Training Centre (RTC) as an active Senior Instructor 

Desirable: 

  • Windsurf/Dinghy Instructor (opposite to SI qualification) 

  • Multihull/Racing/Advanced/Keelboat Instructor endorsements 

  • Wing Instructor 

  • Powerboat Instructor 

  • Good racing knowledge 

  • Knowledge of youth racing pathways 

  • Experience in providing safety cover for racing events 

  • PaddleUK Instructor  

Duties & Responsibilities: 

  • Work as part of a team to provide an enjoyable and safe environment for students and members of all ages and abilities 

  • Support the Duty Officer in ensuring high standards of on-water safety and tuition 

  • Assist the Sailing Principal and Chief Instructors with the day-to-day running of the centre 

  • Lead the instructor team in setting up/packing down the centre daily 

  • Ensure all teaching complies with the RYA curriculum 

  • Deliver RYA courses within the National & Youth Schemes 

  • Assist in managing and maintaining the training fleet 

  • Act as a role model for instructors and assist in their development through mentoring and session monitoring 

  • Train volunteers and Assistant Instructors 

  • Provide on-water safety for recreational and racing activities 

  • Maintain kit and grounds 

  • Youth development 

  • Powerboat training (staff and volunteer skill development)

  • Advanced coaching
  • Keelboat training

 

ACTIVITY INSTRUCTOR

Dinghy, Windsurf, or Powerboat

Casual (throughout the year) and seasonal (June-September 2026)

 

Queen Mary Sailing Club are looking for enthusiastic & passionate Instructors to assist in the delivery of our Training Programme for the coming season. 

Qualifications 

  • Minimum of RYA Dinghy and/or Powerboat or Start Windsurf Instructor or Wing Instructor

 

The main duty will be the delivery of the National & Youth RYA syllabus inline with your qualification and experience. Additional roles include: 

  • in-house training to deliver sit on-top kayaking, stand-up paddleboarding and water/land based team building games 

  • Assisting in the safety provisions for Club & Open racing 

  • Maintenance in the training kit 

  • Launch/Recovery of club members

 

Staff benefits: 

  • Competitive wage

  • Comprehensive training & induction programme including opportunities to up-skill with professional qualifications  

  • Access to our full range of kit (over 100 boats and 50 windsurf boards) in personal time 

  • Discounts on CPD and further instructor courses dependent on amount worked

  • Full & varied role!
